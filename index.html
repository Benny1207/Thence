Daily Wellness Check-In Module Design

Benadin Benny - Associate Product Consultant Role Assignment at Thence.

This report outlines an approach to designing a lightweight, adaptive module that feels like a quick "pulse check" rather than a chore. The core philosophy is to personalize without complexity, keeping the interaction under 60 seconds and using micro-rewards to build strong habit loops.

End-to-End User Flow

Click here to view visualization

1 Entry Trigger (0-5s)

User opens dashboard or taps notification. A motivational prompt card appears, inviting them to start the check-in.

2 Mood Snapshot (10s)

A universal first step using a single-tap emoji wheel and an optional intensity slider to baseline the session.

3 Persona-Tailored Questions (20-30s)

Fitness User

"How was your movement today?" Auto-pulls from wearables or offers tap options.

Nutrition User

"Quick meal vibe?" Suggests options based on prior logs and a water intake toggle.

Mental Well-Being

"Stress level on a 1-5 scale?" with options for what helped or didn't.

Beginner User

"One win today?" A simple, single question to avoid intimidation.

4 Reflection & Insight (10s)

Auto-generates a 1-sentence insight based on trends. User can give feedback to refine future tailoring.

5 Close & Reward (5s)

"Done! Streak: 5 days 🔥" with a confetti burst. Saves to journal and updates dashboard widgets.

6 Post-Flow & Edge Cases

Incomplete inputs are saved as drafts. Missed check-ins trigger gentle reminders or a "Catch-up Week" summary.

Unique Engagement Ideas

Echo Backstreaks

Visualize streaks as a growing "personal echo" waveform with a 3s audio recap. Boosts stickiness by making progress auditory.

Win Weaver

Weaves user input into a shareable "win thread" image for social media, driving re-engagement via social proof.

Phantom Prompts

Injects an AR breathing guide for high-stress moments, using the phone's camera for an immersive, 10s wind-down.

Buddy Sync

Opt-in pairing with a "wellness buddy" to send anonymized, encouraging updates, enhancing retention through accountability.

Supporting Notes

Feature Prioritization (MoSCoW)

Must-Have

Mood snapshot, 1 persona question, draft saving.

Should-Have

Personalized insights, streaks.

Could-Have

AR prompts, buddy sync.

Won't-Have (for now)

Full journaling (defer to separate module).

Metrics for Success

  • Engagement: Completion rate >70%
  • Retention: 7-day streak adoption >40%
  • Stickiness: DAU/MAU >30%
  • Business: Correlate streak users with premium upsell.
