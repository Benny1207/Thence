Engaging & Unique Ideas

Four innovative features designed to make the module delightful, sticky, and highly retentive, all feasible with standard mobile development.

Echo Backstreaks

Visualize progress as a growing "echo" waveform. Tapping it gives a 3s audio recap, making progress feel more tangible.

Win Weaver

Automatically combines daily inputs into a beautiful, shareable image card for social media, boosting organic growth.

Phantom Prompts

Uses AR to offer an immersive, 10-second breathing exercise via the phone's camera when high stress is detected.

Buddy Sync

Creates gentle accountability by letting users share anonymized progress with an opt-in wellness buddy.
